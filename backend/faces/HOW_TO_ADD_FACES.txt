  UWSD Face Database — How to Add Known Persons
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FOLDER STRUCTURE
─────────────────
faces/
  ├── Aarav_Sharma/          ← one folder per person (use underscore for spaces)
  │     ├── photo1.jpg
  │     ├── photo2.jpg       ← multiple photos = better accuracy
  │     └── photo3.jpg
  │
  ├── Priya_Patel/
  │     └── priya.jpg
  │
  └── Your_Name/
        └── selfie.jpg

HOW TO ADD A PERSON
────────────────────
1. Create a new folder inside  faces/  named  FirstName_LastName
   (use underscores, not spaces)

2. Copy 1–5 clear front-facing photos of the person into that folder.
   Supported formats: .jpg  .jpeg  .png

3. Restart the backend  (or it will auto-detect on next WebSocket frame).
   The system rebuilds its recognition index automatically.

TIPS FOR BETTER ACCURACY
─────────────────────────
• Use well-lit, front-facing photos
• Avoid sunglasses or heavy makeup in registration photos
• 3–5 photos per person gives much better recognition than 1
• Photos should show the face clearly (at least 100×100 pixels)
• Mix lighting conditions if possible (indoor + outdoor)

REMOVING A PERSON
──────────────────
Simply delete their folder from  faces/  and restart the backend.
You can also use the Delete button in the Live Feed UI.
